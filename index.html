About VZNQX – Vision Network Quantum Exchange

VZNQX (Vision Network Quantum Exchange), founded in 2018 and headquartered in Chicago, is a next-generation digital asset trading platform built by former Wall Street quantitative architects and early cryptography pioneers.

Our mission is to solve fragmentation in liquidity and network latency across global crypto markets. Powered by our proprietary Q-Core ultra-low latency matching engine and quantum-resistant cryptographic infrastructure, VZNQX delivers institutional-grade performance and security.

Global Expansion & Compliance

In 2024, VZNQX established its European regulatory hub in Zug, Switzerland, widely known as Crypto Valley. In 2026, we achieved a major milestone by obtaining the prestigious BitLicense from the New York State Department of Financial Services (NYDFS), completing our compliance bridge between North America and Europe.
