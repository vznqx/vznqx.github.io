What Are zk-SNARKs?

A privacy-preserving proof system.

Definition

zk-SNARK stands for Zero-Knowledge Succinct Non-Interactive Argument of Knowledge — a cryptographic method that proves the truth of a statement without revealing any underlying data.

Use Cases in Crypto

  • Proof of Reserves: verify solvency without exposing user balances
  • Private transactions on-chain
  • Scalable Layer 2 rollups

VZNQX & zk-SNARKs

VZNQX uses zk-SNARKs-based Proof of Reserves to provide real-time, verifiable proof of user funds with continuous third-party auditing.
