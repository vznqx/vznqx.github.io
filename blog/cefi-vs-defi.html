CeFi vs DeFi: Which Is Better?

Each model has advantages depending on user needs.

Centralized Exchanges (CeFi)

Offer high liquidity, fast execution, and customer support — ideal for institutional and high-volume traders.

Decentralized Exchanges (DeFi)

Provide full user custody and permissionless access, but often suffer from lower liquidity and higher slippage.

The Hybrid Approach

VZNQX combines the speed and depth of CeFi with the transparency and sovereignty of DeFi — the best of both worlds.
