What Is Deep Liquidity in Crypto Trading?

Liquidity determines how fast and efficiently assets can be traded.

Benefits

  • Lower slippage
  • Better execution
  • Tighter bid-ask spreads
  • Reduced market impact for large orders

What Makes Liquidity "Deep"?

Deep liquidity means large volumes of buy and sell orders exist at prices close to the current market price. This allows traders to execute large positions without significantly moving the market.
